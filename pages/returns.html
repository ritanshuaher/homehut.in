Return Policy

Last updated: March 2026

All Sales Are Final

All sales at HomeHut are final. We currently do not accept returns or exchanges once an order has been placed and confirmed. Please review your order carefully before completing your purchase.

Why No Returns?

As a growing business, we keep our prices low by minimizing operational costs. Our no-return policy allows us to pass those savings on to you with better prices and faster delivery.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you received a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the product and packaging. We will resolve the issue promptly — either by sending you a replacement or issuing a full refund.

How to Report an Issue

  1. Email us at homehut09@gmail.com with your order number
  2. Include clear photos of the product and any damage

  4. We will respond within 24 hours
