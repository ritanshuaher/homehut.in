Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

When you visit HomeHut or make a purchase, we collect: name, email address, phone number, shipping address, payment information (processed securely by our payment partners), and browsing behavior on our site.

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Send promotional emails (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and user experience

Data Security

We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions. Your payment details are never stored on our servers. We partner with PCI-compliant payment gateways.

Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, and personalize your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We share information only with: shipping partners (to deliver your orders), payment processors (to process transactions), and analytics tools (anonymized data only).

Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data, request corrections, request deletion, opt out of marketing emails, and export your data. Contact us at homehut09@gmail.com.
